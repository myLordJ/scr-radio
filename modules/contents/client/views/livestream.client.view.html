ABOUT US

Operating out of a bunker studio in Itaewon’s multicultural hotbed Usadan-ro, Seoul Community Radio (SCR) is ON AIR and on a mission. Created by the scene for the people that make it great, SCR exists to champion underground culture in Korea and provide the LIVE audio-visual platform to help it grow and thrive. Music, Creative works, Events + Community. Freshness made in Seoul, with the World invited.
